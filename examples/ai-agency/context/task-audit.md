## Automation Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Requires human judgment, creativity, or relationship — hard to automate |
| 2 | Partially automatable — AI can assist but human oversight needed |
| 3 | Mostly automatable with good prompts and templates |
| 4 | Highly automatable — clear process, repetitive, pattern-based |
| 5 | Fully automatable — schedule it and forget it |
