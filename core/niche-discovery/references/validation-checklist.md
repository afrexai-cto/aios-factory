# Niche Validation Checklist

Use this checklist when evaluating a new business vertical for an AIOS workspace.

---

## Pre-Research

- [ ] Vertical name clearly defined (specific, not vague)
- [ ] Target geography identified (country or region)
- [ ] Target business size identified (solo, small, medium, enterprise)
- [ ] Initial hypothesis for why this vertical needs an AIOS

---

## Criterion 1: Market Size

- [ ] Estimated number of businesses in target geography
- [ ] Source for the estimate documented
- [ ] Addressable segment identified (not every business in the vertical)
- [ ] Score assigned: Low / Medium / High

**Notes:** _______________________________________________

---

## Criterion 2: Pain Point Severity

- [ ] At least 2 pain points identified
- [ ] For each pain point: frequency documented (daily, weekly, per-client)
- [ ] For each pain point: consequence of not solving documented
- [ ] Evidence that businesses would pay to solve this (existing tools, complaints)
- [ ] Score assigned: Low / Medium / High

**Notes:** _______________________________________________

---

## Criterion 3: Regulatory Environment

- [ ] Primary regulator identified (or confirmed: no regulator)
- [ ] Key compliance obligations listed
- [ ] Periodic reporting or audit requirements identified
- [ ] Personal liability implications assessed
- [ ] Score assigned: Low / Medium / High

**Notes:** _______________________________________________

---

## Criterion 4: Technology Adoption

- [ ] Common software tools in the vertical identified
- [ ] Typical IT setup described (in-house, managed, none)
- [ ] Cloud tool adoption level assessed
- [ ] Barriers to adoption identified (security concerns, training needs)
- [ ] Score assigned: Low / Medium / High

**Notes:** _______________________________________________

---

## Criterion 5: Workflow Complexity

- [ ] At least 2 core workflows mapped end-to-end
- [ ] Number of steps, decisions, and handoffs counted
- [ ] Bottlenecks and failure points identified
- [ ] Workflow documentation exists (or was created during research)
- [ ] Score assigned: Low / Medium / High

**Notes:** _______________________________________________

---

## Criterion 6: Community Density

- [ ] Professional associations identified
- [ ] Online communities found (LinkedIn, forums, industry groups)
- [ ] Conference or meetup culture assessed
- [ ] Referral behavior evaluated (do they share tools?)
- [ ] Score assigned: Low / Medium / High

**Notes:** _______________________________________________

---

## Criterion 7: Automation Potential

- [ ] Most time-consuming tasks identified
- [ ] Pattern-based tasks flagged (AI can learn patterns)
- [ ] Tasks requiring encoded judgment identified
- [ ] Speed or accuracy bottleneck identified
- [ ] Score assigned: Low / Medium / High
